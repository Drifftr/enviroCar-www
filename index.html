Join the enviroCar Community – be a Citizen Scientist!

enviroCar invites you to use your car’s sensor data for exploring the effect of your driving style on environmental aspects such as fuel consumption, CO2 production or noise emission. Compare your driving statistics with friends and compete to become the most efficient driver, and to reduce your car’s operating costs!
Share your data with the enviroCar citizen science community and collaborate with other citizens, scientists and urban planners to ask and answer more questions such as: what are the actual spatial characteristics of fuel consumption in your city, how to optimize traffic planning to reduce emissions effectively, or what to learn from average speed maps...
enviroCar provides an extremely valuable new resource of environmental information. Join the community and help to make our world a little smarter, traffic less stressful, and economic driving more fun!
